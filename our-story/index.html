Our Story

Every Home of Linen product begins with over 30 years of Egyptian textile expertise.

Founded by Nourytex, a trusted manufacturer established in 1988, our heritage is built on crafting premium home textiles for many of Egypt’s leading brands.

Today, we’re proud to bring that same quality directly from our factory to your home—offering beautifully crafted bedding, towels, and home essentials made from the finest Egyptian cotton, designed for everyday comfort and made to last.
