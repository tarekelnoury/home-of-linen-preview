Egyptian Cotton Flat Sheets

Calm, premium essentials for Egyptian homes.

Egyptian cotton flat sheets bring a light, elegant layer to the bed. They can be folded neatly over the duvet, used as a breathable top sheet or paired with fitted sheets for a complete hotel-inspired set. Home of Linen flat sheet sets are made for customers who enjoy a polished bed with a soft, calm finish. Available in considered colours, each flat sheet set can match your fitted sheet, pillowcases and duvet cover for a coordinated bedroom. Flat sheets are also useful for guest rooms, summer sleeping and bridal trousseau bundles because they add flexibility without visual clutter.
